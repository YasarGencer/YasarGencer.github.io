Endless Warrior

ENDLESS-ACTION-PLATFORMER

Play Store Itch.io YouTube

Endless Warrior is an endless runner game set in the middle ages. Goal is to get as many point as possible before dying or falling down off the platforms. To reach your goal you must use the two mechanics very presisly right.

As you can tell by now game has only two player controlled mechanics. Sliding and jumping. Both of them are designed the same. If you press longer you jump/slide longer. This mechanics includes physics as well. For example if you jump while sliding you will jump much longer because of your speed.

Enviroment is randomly generated each run to keep player more entertained. This every run feels slightly different than other one.
