Hook Castle

ENDLESS-RUNNER

Play Store

Hook Castle is an endless runner game where player tries to reach to the top of the castle. Player has a to throw a hook to get to the higher platforms.

Gameplay is as simple as posible. Player only needs to touch the screen when the indicator directs to the next platform. Then the hook will be throwen to get player up.

There is a quite variety when it comes to the platform types. There is more than 4 simple platforms and 2 moving, 2 smaller and 2 ice platforms that throw ice projectiles which froze player. When frozen player has to tap 3 time sto become unfrozen.
