Project-A

ACTION-PLATFORMER

Itch.io

Project-A is an 2d action-platformer game. Main goal is fighting towards the exit of the vault player is in. After every vault player will be forced to fight against the boss of the vault.

As the game progress player will encounter lots of bosses and different vault to explore and conquer. While player roams around different vaults themes will change from vault to vault. For example, in one vault player will be in a happy colorfull place with full of life, in another vault player will be in a distopic city.

With the theme changes through vaults, gameplay elements would change as well. For example, in one vault player will have to find and protect the light source to stay away from darkness, in another vault it will have to find its way out of the maze.
